Travel Couple from Kerala

We are passionate travelers exploring hidden gems, beautiful destinations, luxury stays, local food experiences, road trips, and unforgettable adventures across India. Every journey we take is a chapter waiting to be written — and this is where we share those stories.

From the misty tea gardens of Munnar to the golden sands of Varkala, from silent forest paths in Wayanad to the shimmering backwaters of Alleppey — our chronicles are born on the road.

Why We Started Panvi Chronicles

Panvi Chronicles was born from a simple dream — to document our travels, share hidden destinations, inspire fellow travelers, and preserve memories from every road we take. What started as weekend adventures has become a journey of storytelling, photography, and exploration.
